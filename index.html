MAF

Mohammed Ahnaf Firsan (born March 10, 2011), widely known as MAF, is a Sri Lankan novelist, content creator, and digital personality.

Career

MAF is known for his work in digital content creation and original storytelling. He develops superhero-based narrative concepts with a focus on structured storytelling and character development.

He is also active as a content creator, building a growing online presence across multiple platforms under the name MAF.

Works

The Echolon: The Beginning – A superhero concept universe featuring original characters and narrative arcs.
